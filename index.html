Hi, I am Zhuangbin Chen, currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Software Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University. I work on Software Engineering, Networking, and Cloud Computing. More specifically, I am interested in improving the reliability of online services and the troubleshooting process of data center networks. Prior to SYSU, I obtained a Ph.D. at the Computer Science and Engineering Department of The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I was privileged to work with Prof. Michael R. Lyu.
